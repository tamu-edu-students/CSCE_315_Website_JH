QUALIFICATIONS

Although I'm pursuing a degree in computer science, my technical areas of interest are almost all cybersecurity related. I like identifying vulnerabilities in systems and creating exploits, since most vulnerabilities differ from others enough to make it a creative process with different requirements every time. I really enjoy being able to take broad knowledge of computer systems and programs and find unique and interesting ways to apply that knowledge to break a system, or bypass its security. Other than security, I'm also quite interesting in artificial intelligence and machine learning, although I haven't found the courage to tinker with those yet.

As you can see in the resume, most of my technical skills are offensive security related. I have lots of experience exploiting web applications by exploiting technologies such as Python, Flask, Jinja, SQL, and more. I have experience with Wi-Fi and bluetooth hacking, creating malware for Windows systems, and I'm proficient with linux environments. I'm also proficient with many tools used for offensive security, such as Kali Linux, Wireshark , Burpsuite, and more.

I also have lots of experience using tools like Splunk, Crowdstrike, Extrahop, and more in an enterprise environment. I'm a proficient threat hunter, and have experience patching, securing, and hardening many types of systems. I'm good at working in team environments, and possess a security oriented mindset for both defensive and offensive operations.
